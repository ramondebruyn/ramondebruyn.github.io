Policy Terms

We are committed to protecting your privacy. We have created this Privacy Policy to show our commitment to the protection of your privacy while you use Infinite Counter+ (the “App”) and any services available through or in connection with the App. Your use of the App is covered by this Privacy Policy and by the Terms of Use.


1. WHAT THIS POLICY COVERS

This Privacy Policy is applicable only to the App.


2. PERSONAL INFORMATION WE COLLECT AUTOMATICALLY

We do not collect any personal data, neither is the use of this app contingent upon any collection thereof. This applies to all users, regardless of their nationality. Specifically, if you are an individual in the European Union (EU) or an EU citizen, this app is provided as an educational tool, and no personal information required, collected or stored.


3. LINKS TO OTHER APPLICATIONS

Our App does not present any type of advertisements or Third-Party advertisements and does not contain links to other applications and services with which Infinite Counter+ is not affiliated (“Third-Party Apps”).


4. DATA RETENTION

To be clear. We do not collect or retain any of your data. We believe that your or your child’s access to education should not be subject to any tradeoffs that include you or your child giving up any personal information. Education is a universal right, and should not be contingent upon one’s belief or stance on right to privacy.


5. DATA TRANSFER AND STORAGE

The App is stored on servers in the United Kingdom (UK). Thus, if you are outside of the EU, the Personal Information that you provide to Da Vinci shall be transferred to the UK. Upon providing your Personal Information, you are agreeing both to the transfer of your Personal Information to the UK and to the storage of your Personal Information in the UK, as well as to the use of your Personal Information in accordance with the ends for which it was originally collected. The data that we collect from you may be transferred to, and stored at, a destination outside the European Economic Area (“EEA“). When this occurs we will take steps to ensure your information receives an equivalent level of protection to those as in the EEA. We will take all steps reasonably necessary to ensure that your data is treated securely and in accordance with this privacy policy and applicable Data Protection Laws.


6. CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY CONTRACT

We reserve the right to alter this Privacy Policy at any moment and at our sole discretion. In the event of any changes to this Privacy Policy, we shall publish an altered agreement in the App that will inform you of these changes. We will obtain prior parental consent if any changes we make to this Policy affect users under the age of 16 in a way that requires such consent under COPPA.


7. CONTACT US

If you have any questions, please submit your question and contact details on the Contact page, and we'll back to you.
