Intuitive Interface for Curious Minds


Aimed at 3-7 year-olds, the goal was to make a simple, fun app that combined auditory and visual learning for young kids.

Build to support various screen sizes, and with support for English, Afrikaans, Spanish, and French, the aim was to make the app as accessible as possible.
